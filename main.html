How it works

Up and running in seconds.

1

Download & Install

Download SpotOn and drag it to your Applications folder. Launch it and it'll appear in your menu bar.

2

Grant Accessibility Permission

Allow SpotOn to use Accessibility features so it can detect your keyboard shortcut system-wide.

3

Set Your Shortcut

Choose your preferred hotkey combination from Settings. Default is ⌥ + S.

4

Focus & Work

Press your shortcut anytime to toggle the spotlight overlay and stay focused on what matters.
